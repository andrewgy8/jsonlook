jsonlook

Simple, safe and secure offline viewer for your json data


Why did I build this?

Simply put, I was tired of sketchy online json viewers. I wanted something that does its job AND without obfuscating what its doing with your data.

We use pure vanilla-js, which means you can read all the code.
